## Overview

This directory holds internal department budgets, sales goal forecasting models, and long-term liquidity plans.

### Department Budgets:
- Delivery and Onboarding Operations
- Sales & Marketing Spend (Paid ad budgets, outbounding tool budgets)
- Infrastructure Expenses (Agent tokens, AI API bounds)

### Forecast Iterations:
- **Baseline Forecasting:** Using historic conversion tracking.
- **Aggressive Outbound Scenarios:** Stress testing the model based on doubling marketing spend.
- **Hiring Roadmap:** Connecting runway capacity to new headcount triggers.
