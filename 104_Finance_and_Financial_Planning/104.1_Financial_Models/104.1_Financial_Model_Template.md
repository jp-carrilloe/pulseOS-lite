## Overview

This directory acts as the centralized point for financial modeling in the company.
A key function is tying operational assumptions (CAC, conversion rates, hiring schedules) to financial outputs (runway, net margin, LTV).

### Key Variables to Track:
- **Revenue Targets:** MRR, ARR growth trajectories.
- **Unit Economics:** Acquisition costs, onboarding costs, customer lifespan (churn rate).
- **OpEx:** Software costs (AI infrastructure, SaaS, Cloud), sales commissions, legal fees.
- **Runway Scenarios:** Base Case, Best Case, Worst Case models based on current burn rate.
