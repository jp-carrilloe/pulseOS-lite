## Overview
This directory contains all the financial logic, models, forecasts, and budgeting templates for the company. The goal of this structured repository is to ensure that financial health and unit economics are easily accessible and integrated into strategic decision-making.

## Subdirectories

### `104.1_Financial_Models`
Contains the master financial models, pricing calculators, and unit economics breakdowns (LTV, CAC, payback periods).

### `104.2_Budgets_and_Forecasts`
Contains annual and quarterly budgets, cash flow projections, run-rate scenarios, and hiring plans.

### `12.3_P_and_L_Statements`
Contains standard Profit & Loss statement templates, historic tracking models, and expense categorization rules.
