## 2. AI Agent Compliance

[CLIENT_NAME] deploys AI agents that interact with third parties, process data, and take automated actions. The following rules govern agent behaviour and associated compliance obligations:

### Agent Behaviour Guardrails

| Rule | Detail |
|---|---|
| Transparency | Where agents interact externally (e.g., outreach sequences), disclose AI involvement where required by law |
| No Deceptive Practices | Agents must not impersonate humans in a way that misleads recipients |
| Data Minimisation | Agents should only process the data necessary for their defined task |
| Output Review | High-stakes agent outputs (contracts, financial decisions, legal filings) require human review before action |
| Consent | Outreach agents must operate within applicable email and messaging consent laws (CAN-SPAM, CASL, GDPR) |

### Applicable Regulations for AI Actions

| Regulation | Jurisdiction | Applies To |
|---|---|---|
| EU AI Act | EU | High-risk AI systems (TBC by use case) |
| CAN-SPAM | USA | Commercial email sent to US recipients |
| CASL | Canada | Commercial electronic messages to Canadian recipients |
| GDPR Article 22 | EU | Automated decision-making with significant effects on individuals |

## 5. Regulatory Monitoring

The following changes in law or regulation must trigger a review of this document and relevant contracts:

- New or amended data protection legislation in operating markets
- AI regulation developments (EU AI Act implementation, national AI laws)
- Changes to email/outreach consent laws
- Employment law changes in any jurisdiction where staff or contractors are based

**Review Cadence:** Quarterly review by Legal Agent. Immediate review on any regulatory event.
