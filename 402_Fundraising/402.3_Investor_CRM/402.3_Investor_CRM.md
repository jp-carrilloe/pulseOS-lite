## Pipeline Stage Definitions

1. **Targeting** — Investor thesis, stage, and check-size fit confirmed
2. **Warm Intro Requested** — Intro path activated through founder, advisor, or portfolio contact
3. **Outreach Sent** — Intro email or direct outreach delivered
4. **First Meeting** — Initial pitch + qualification completed
5. **Partner Meeting** — Decision-maker meeting scheduled / completed
6. **Due Diligence** — Data room opened and diligence in progress
7. **Term Sheet** — Commercial terms under negotiation
8. **Closed** — Signed documents + funds wired
