## Page 3: The Playbook Revealed (Transparency)
**Headline**: The Blueprint for Reliable Agent Operations.  
**Visual**: A clear, replicable 4-step architecture: 102. Strategic Context Model -> 201. Evidence and Workflow Mapping -> 202. Grounded Access and Simulation Design -> 203. Operational Deployment Blueprint.

**The Strategy:** Give away the "Secret Sauce." When they see exactly how much work goes into creating a high-converting flow, they will realize they want us to just build it for them.

*   **1. Strategic Context Model**: Define the operating model, key workflows, systems, constraints, and decision surfaces that matter most.
*   **2. Evidence and Workflow Mapping**: Turn fragmented documents, tools, ownership, and process rules into a canonical company memory layer with provenance.
*   **3. Grounded Access and Simulation Design**: Structure how humans and agents query the memory layer, what they are allowed to access, and how proposed actions are tested safely before execution.
*   **4. Operational Deployment Blueprint**: Translate the model into a practical rollout plan covering interfaces, approvals, security, and the first production use case.

> **Script**: "The hard truth is that prompt engineering and disconnected automations are no longer enough. The blueprint is 4 steps: strategic context model, evidence and workflow mapping, grounded access and simulation design, and operational deployment blueprint. We define the operating model, normalize the evidence, design the access and simulation layer, and then orchestrate this so the company's agent readiness becomes organic. Because we give the company a reusable memory and grounding layer, one of your team members can now do the work of an entire traditional coordination layer."

## Page 4: System Diagnosis (Collaborative Discovery)
**Headline**: Mapping Your Current Architecture.  
**Visual**: A clean diagnostic flowchart looking at context fragmentation, workflow ambiguity, and execution risk. Checkboxes for "Manual vs. Automated."

**The Strategy:** Turn the mirror on their operation. Act as a consultant diagnosing systemic blockers.

**Consultative Questions**:
102. **Context Layer:** "Where does critical company knowledge live today, and how often does your team have to reconstruct it manually before work can move?"
201. **Workflow Layer:** "Which recurring workflows break down because ownership, approvals, or dependencies are spread across too many systems?"
202. **Execution Layer:** "If an AI agent proposed a real action tomorrow, what would stop you from trusting it to run without heavy human checking?"

> **Script**: "To figure out if our specific infrastructure would actually help you—or if you're fine as is—let's map out your current flow. Where do you feel your team is losing the most hours right now?"
