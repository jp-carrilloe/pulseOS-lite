## 203.1. Purpose / Objective
The **Sales Enablement Hub** is the operational domain responsible for bridging the gap between GTM Strategy and revenue execution. It serves as the central repository for customer-facing assets, automated outbound logic, and the internal knowledge base required to scale the sales motion.
