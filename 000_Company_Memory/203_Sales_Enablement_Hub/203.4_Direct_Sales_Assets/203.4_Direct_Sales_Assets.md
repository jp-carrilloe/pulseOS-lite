## 5. Monthly Review Protocol

### Review Cadence
| Review Type | Frequency | Format | Duration |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Weekly Pulse | Weekly | Async (written or recorded) | 5–10 min |
| Monthly Deep Dive | Monthly | Live call | 45–60 min |
| Quarterly Strategy Session | Quarterly | Live call + deck | 90 min |

### Monthly Review Agenda
1. **Performance Metrics (15 min):** Outreach volume, open/reply/meeting rates, pipeline generated, CRM health
2. **What Worked (10 min):** Top-performing sequence, best-converting segment, unexpected wins
3. **What Didn't Work (10 min):** Lowest-performing segment, recurring objections, blockers
4. **Optimization Decisions (10 min):** Data-driven changes, scope escalations, client approvals needed
5. **Next 30 Days (10 min):** Updated priorities, new signals or ICPs, resource dependencies
