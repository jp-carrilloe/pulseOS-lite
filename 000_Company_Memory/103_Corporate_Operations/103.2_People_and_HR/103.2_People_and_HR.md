## 3. Onboarding Checklist

### Pre-Start (Before Day 1)
- [ ] Contract signed and filed
- [ ] Equipment ordered / access credentials prepared
- [ ] Tool access provisioned (see stack in `103.1_Company_Setup`)
- [ ] Welcome email sent with Day 1 agenda

### Week 1
- [ ] Company overview and mission briefing
- [ ] Introduction to team and key stakeholders
- [ ] Access to relevant knowledge base sections confirmed
- [ ] First task or project assigned

### Month 1
- [ ] 30-day check-in scheduled
- [ ] Role expectations confirmed and written down
- [ ] Feedback loop established

---

## 4. Performance Management

### Review Cadence
- **Monthly:** Informal check-in (15–30 min)
- **Quarterly:** Structured review against role KPIs
- **Annually:** Compensation and role review

### Performance Issues
- Document concerns in writing immediately
- Follow jurisdiction-specific process from `106_Legal_and_Compliance/106.3_Regulatory_and_Compliance/`
- Escalate to @ARK before any formal action

---

## 5. Offboarding Checklist

- [ ] Written notice received / issued per contract terms
- [ ] Final pay and any outstanding invoices calculated
- [ ] All tool and system access revoked
- [ ] Company equipment returned
- [ ] Knowledge transfer completed
- [ ] Exit interview conducted (optional but recommended)
- [ ] Files and work archived
