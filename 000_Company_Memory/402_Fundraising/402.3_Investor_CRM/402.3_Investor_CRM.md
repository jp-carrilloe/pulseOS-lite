# Investor CRM & Relationship Tracker — PulseOS-KB

## Purpose
Tracks selective investor relationships during PulseOS-KB's stealth founder-formation stage. The CRM is optimized for talent introductions, strategic value-add, anchor-angel filtering, and warming the right investors for a later formal pre-seed.

## Pipeline Stage Definitions

1. **Targeting** — Investor identified; category and network fit confirmed
2. **Intro Requested** — Warm path activated
3. **Intro / First Conversation** — Initial conversation completed or scheduled
4. **Talent Value-Add** — Investor actively helping with founder introductions or recruiting
5. **Anchor Angel Interest** — Investor has expressed credible interest in a small early allocation
6. **Warm for Formal Round** — Strong future-round fit; relationship kept warm for formal pre-seed
7. **Closed / Deferred** — Relationship parked, passed, or converted to a later-stage follow-up

## What to Track Per Investor

- talent network strength
- relevance to one or more of the three target cofounder roles
- anchor-angel fit
- future pre-seed fit
- any conflict or sensitivity
