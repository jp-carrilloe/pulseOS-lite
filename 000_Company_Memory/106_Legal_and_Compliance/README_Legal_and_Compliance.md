## Purpose

The **hard boundary layer** of **[CLIENT_NAME]**. This section defines the legal frameworks, contract templates, IP ownership rules, regulatory requirements, and risk protocols that govern every external commitment and internal practice. ## Structure

### 106.1 Contracts and Agreements
- Master Service Agreement (MSA) — standard client contract template
- Statement of Work (SOW) — per-engagement scope and payment template
- Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) — mutual and one-way variants
- Partnership and Referral Agreements — for `401_Strategic_Partnerships`

### 106.2 Intellectual Property
- IP ownership framework: what the company owns vs. what clients own
- Agent-created IP clauses — who owns outputs generated by AI agents
- Trademark and copyright guidance
- Work-for-hire vs. licence-to-use distinction

### 106.3 Regulatory and Compliance
- GDPR and CCPA compliance requirements
- Jurisdiction-specific rules for operating markets
- Agent behaviour guardrails (liability for AI-driven actions)
- Data Processing Agreements (DPA) — referenced by `105_Technical_Infrastructure_and_Security`

### 106.4 Risk and Liability
- Liability framework for agent failure scenarios
- Insurance requirements and coverage review checklist
- Dispute resolution protocols
- Escalation matrix for legal risk events
