## 1. Purpose and Order of Precedence
**1.1** The Client intends to commission the Service Provider with services described in a mutually agreed Statement of Work (SOW) or Service Order appended to this Agreement.
**1.2** In the event of a conflict between this Agreement and a specific SOW, the provisions of the SOW shall prevail on matters of scope and payment; this Agreement shall prevail on all other matters.

## 2. Terms of Collaboration
**2.1** The Service Provider shall perform contracted services in a proper, timely, and professional manner in accordance with agreed requirements.
**2.2** The Parties shall communicate at regular intervals regarding progress and any potential obstacles.
**2.3** The Service Provider acts as an independent contractor. This Agreement does not create an employment relationship between the Parties.

## 3. Client Obligations
**3.1** The Client shall provide the Service Provider with all data, system access, and information necessary to perform the agreed services in a timely manner.
**3.2** The Client shall grant required approvals promptly so that the Service Provider's workflow is not impaired.
**3.3** Where the Client provides materials or content, the Client warrants that these are free of third-party rights and do not violate applicable law.

## 4. Remuneration and Payment Terms
**4.1** Specific remuneration for services is defined in the applicable SOW.
**4.2** All fees are subject to statutory taxes applicable at the time of invoicing.
**4.3** Invoices are due for payment within [NUMBER] days of receipt. Late payments accrue interest at [RATE]% per month on the outstanding balance.
**4.4** Third-party service costs (APIs, software, data providers) incurred on behalf of the Client are either borne directly by the Client or passed through at cost with prior notification.

## 5. Intellectual Property and Rights of Use
**5.1** Upon full payment of the agreed remuneration, the Service Provider grants the Client exclusive, irrevocable rights to use all custom deliverables produced under this Agreement, unlimited in territory and time.
**5.2** The Service Provider retains ownership of all pre-existing tools, frameworks, methodologies, and generic AI agent infrastructure used to produce deliverables.
**5.3** Any deliverables produced using AI agents or automated systems remain subject to this clause — the Client owns the outputs, the Service Provider retains the infrastructure.
**5.4** The Service Provider retains the right to reference the engagement and use the Client's logo in its own portfolio and marketing, unless explicitly agreed otherwise in writing.

## 6. Confidentiality
**6.1** Both Parties shall treat all information regarding the other Party's business operations, clients, systems, and strategic plans as strictly confidential.
**6.2** This obligation survives termination of this Agreement for a period of [NUMBER] years.
**6.3** Confidentiality does not apply to information that is publicly available, independently developed, or required to be disclosed by law.

## 7. Liability
**7.1** The Parties shall be liable to each other in accordance with statutory provisions for all damage culpably caused to the other Party.
**7.2** The Service Provider's total aggregate liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by the Client in the [NUMBER] months preceding the event giving rise to the claim.
**7.3** The Service Provider shall not be held liable for third-party service failures (e.g., API outages, platform policy changes, data provider errors).
**7.4** Neither Party shall be liable for indirect, consequential, or punitive damages arising from performance or non-performance under this Agreement.

## 8. Data Protection
**8.1** Where the Service Provider processes personal data on behalf of the Client, the Parties shall enter into a separate Data Processing Agreement (DPA) in accordance with applicable data protection law (including GDPR where relevant).
**8.2** The Service Provider shall implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or breach.

## 9. Term and Termination
**9.1** This Agreement comes into effect upon signature and remains valid indefinitely until terminated.
**9.2** Either Party may terminate this Agreement with [NUMBER] days written notice, unless a different term is specified in an active SOW.
**9.3** Either Party may terminate immediately in writing if the other Party commits a material breach that is not remedied within [NUMBER] days of written notice.
**9.4** On termination, the Service Provider retains the right to remuneration for all services rendered prior to the effective termination date.

## 10. Final Provisions
**10.1** Amendments and supplements to this Agreement must be made in writing and signed by both Parties.
**10.2** Should any provision of this Agreement be or become invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force.
**10.3** This Agreement is governed by the laws of [JURISDICTION]. The place of jurisdiction for all disputes is [CITY, COUNTRY].
**10.4** The Parties agree to attempt to resolve any dispute through good-faith negotiation before initiating legal proceedings.
