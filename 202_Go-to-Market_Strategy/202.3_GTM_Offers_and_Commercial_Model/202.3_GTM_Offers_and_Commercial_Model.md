## Purpose
Defines the offer architecture, pricing philosophy, and ICP-specific commercialization model. This document ensures that what we sell is packaged in a way that aligns with the prospect's decision-making logic and risk tolerance.

## 102. Offer Philosophy: Infrastructure, Not Subscription

[CLIENT_NAME]'s commercial model is designed around a core principle: **we sell a strategic asset, not a monthly subscription.**

- **Position**: Unlike SaaS tools (charged per seat) or AI SDRs (charged per rep), [CLIENT_NAME] wraps its service as **Fixed Performance Infrastructure** — a system that compounds over time and belongs to the client.
- **Mental Model for Prospects**: "You are hiring an Autonomous Revenue Team, not renting software."
- **Commercial Advantage**: A fixed retainer model decouples our economics from the volume we produce, allowing us to focus on quality and strategic depth instead of throughput.

## 201. Core Offer Architecture

### Offer 1: The 14-Day Sprint (Entry / Pilot)
*The lowest-risk, fastest-proof entry point for new clients.*

- **Scope**: Full outbound system deployment for one ICP segment.
    - Research & Dossier construction (50-100 targeted accounts).
    - Email + LinkedIn sequence activation (Managed by [CLIENT_NAME] agents).
    - CRM integration (HubSpot or Salesforce).
    - Weekly performance review.
    - Qualified Lead Handover: at least 1 meeting booked.
- **Duration**: 14 Days.
- **Pricing**: Fixed Sprint Fee (one-time). Defined in `102_Corporate_Strategy_and_Foundation/102.5_Pricing_Strategy/`.
- **Success Criteria**: At least 1 qualified, meeting-ready lead delivered within 14 days.
- **ICP Fit**: SaaS Founder (P4), VP of Sales (P1) who needs proof before committing.

---

### Offer 2: The GTM Engine (Core Retainer)
*The standard ongoing engagement for scaling companies.*

- **Scope**: Full, ongoing Autonomous GTM Infrastructure.
    - Continuous ICP prospecting and enrichment.
    - Multi-channel outreach (Email + LinkedIn) across multiple ICP segments.
    - Content marketing support and UTM tracking.
    - Full CRM synchronization and data hygiene.
    - AI Research Dossiers on every prospect.
    - Bi-weekly performance reviews and optimization.
    - Qualified Lead Handover pipeline, ongoing.
- **Duration**: Monthly retainer (annual commitment recommended).
- **Pricing**: Custom retainer based on scope (number of ICP segments, number of channels, volume). Defined in `102_Corporate_Strategy_and_Foundation/102.5_Pricing_Strategy/`.
- **ICP Fit**: VP of Sales (P1), Head of RevOps (P2), Growth-stage CEO (P3).

---

### Offer 3: The GTM Architecture Blueprint (Advisory)
*For clients who want to own the system internally or need strategic clarity before committing.*

- **Scope**: A time-boxed consulting engagement.
    - Full Audit of current GTM motion and tech stack.
    - Manual Waste Report delivery.
    - Custom GTM Architecture Map (visual Spine design).
    - Agent logic design and sequence recommendations.
    - Vendor/tool recommendations for independent execution.
- **Duration**: 2-4 week workshop.
- **Pricing**: Fixed project fee. May credit toward GTM Engine retainer.
- **ICP Fit**: Traditional Modernizer (P5), RevOps leads (P2) who need to build an internal case before buying.

## 203. Expansion Model (Land & Expand)

[CLIENT_NAME]'s strategy is to enter through one ICP segment and expand horizontally within the account.

- **Land**: Deploy the GTM Engine for the Sales function (Outbound SDR replacement).
- **Expand Layer 1**: Deploy Support 501_Agents_and_Workflows (Customer success / ticket deflection).
- **Expand Layer 2**: Deploy additional ICP segments (e.g., add Logistics vertical to existing SaaS vertical).
- **Expand Layer 3**: [CLIENT_NAME] becomes the client's internal "AI Operations Team," managing a suite of AI agents across Revenue, Support, and Ops.

## 301. Commercial Guardrails (Non-Negotiables)

- **No pure lead-gen billing model**: Do not offer pricing per lead or per meeting. This commoditizes the infrastructure and creates perverse incentives.
- **No short-term (< 30 day) commitments outside the Sprint**: One-month trial periods are not offered after the Sprint phase.
- **Annual discount is allowed**: An ACV (Annual Contract Value) discount of up to 15% may be offered for annual pre-pay commitments.
- **Pilot-to-Retainer conversion**: The Sprint offer is always positioned as a gateway to the GTM Engine — not a standalone product. Conversion SLAs are tracked in `302_Analytics_and_Performance_Intelligence/`.
