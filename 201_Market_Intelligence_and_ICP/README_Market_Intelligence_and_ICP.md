## Purpose

This folder contains the data and analysis required to identify and target the right customers with high precision. The outputs from this folder feed directly into the Execution Engine's outreach workflows.

## Sub-folders

### 201.1 Target Personas and Segmentation
- **Purpose:** Profiles of ideal buyer personas, segmented by industry, company size, and pain points.
- **Key Asset:** `icp_matrix.md`

### 201.2 Competitive Intelligence
- **Purpose:** Benchmarking against competitors to identify gaps, differentiation opportunities, and positioning edges.
- **Key Asset:** `competitive_landscape.md`

### 201.3 Market Signals and Intent Triggers
- **Purpose:** A catalog of high-intent signals (funding rounds, job postings, technology adoption, etc.) used to trigger autonomous outreach.
- **Key Asset:** `market_signals_and_triggers.md`
