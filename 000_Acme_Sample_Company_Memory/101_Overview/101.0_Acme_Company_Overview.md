## Purpose
This persistent sample memory shows how a populated company operating system can look without being mixed into the main indexed knowledge-base tables. Acme Inc is a fictional B2B operations company kept in the repo for repeatable testing.

## Company Snapshot
Acme Inc helps growing B2B teams standardize sales, onboarding, reporting, and internal operating rhythms through packaged AI-assisted operations services. The company sells to founder-led teams that have customer demand but inconsistent internal systems.

## Operating Model
- Strategy defines the service portfolio and commercial thesis in [Acme Strategy](../102_Strategy/102.1_Acme_Strategy.md).
- Operations turns the thesis into repeatable cadence in [Acme Operations](../103_Operations/103.1_Acme_Operations.md).
- GTM converts the strategy into campaigns in [Acme GTM](../202_GTM/202.1_Acme_GTM_Strategy.md).
- Delivery turns sold work into onboarding and adoption in [Acme Delivery](../301_Delivery/301.1_Acme_Delivery_Model.md).
