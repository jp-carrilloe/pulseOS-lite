## Purpose
Provides sample financial assumptions for Acme Inc so the persistent sample includes measurable business context.

## Revenue Assumptions
Acme Inc sells fixed-scope packages and monthly retainers. The sample model assumes a blended average first engagement of USD 18,000 and a follow-on monthly retainer of USD 6,000 for retained clients.

## Dependencies
Financial assumptions depend on packaging from [Acme Strategy](../102_Strategy/102.1_Acme_Strategy.md) and acquisition volume from [Acme GTM](../202_GTM/202.1_Acme_GTM_Strategy.md).
